== Description ==

**Sell smart from the start with Hello Commerce**

Hello Commerce is a free WordPress ecommerce theme designed to help you launch your online store quickly and professionally. Built for WooCommerce and powered by the flexibility of Elementor, it offers a lightweight, responsive foundation that's perfect for beginners and growing businesses alike. Whether you're selling products or services, the Hello Commerce theme gives you the freedom to build your store your way, launch fast, and scale with confidence. 

Start strong with a guided setup, ready-made shop templates, and intuitive styling controls. Customize your storefront's style and layout, including product grids, cart, and checkout pages, with intuitive controls, directly within the Elementor ecosystem without code. Manage your site efficiently using the dedicated Home screen that brings key tools and actions together.

**Why Hello Commerce?**

- Seamless integration with Elementor and WooCommerce
- Fast, flexible, and performance-optimized
- Beginner-friendly, no code or technical skills required
- Complete site template kits to launch your store quickly

Report security bugs through the Patchstack Vulnerability Disclosure Program. The Patchstack team validates, triages, and handles vulnerabilities. Report here: https://patchstack.com/database

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Who is the Hello Commerce theme for? =
Hello Commerce is designed to help you build professional online shops quickly and efficiently.

= Is Hello Commerce suitable for beginners? =
Absolutely! Hello Commerce is designed for all skill levels, making it the perfect choice for beginners and professionals alike.
- **Beginner-Friendly** – The step-by-step onboarding wizard makes setup effortless.
- **Centralized Home Dashboard** – Easily access key sections of your website from one place.
- **Flexible & Scalable** – While simple to use, Hello Commerce also provides powerful customization options for experienced users looking to build advanced websites.

= Can I use Hello Commerce to build sites for clients? =
Yes! Hello Commerce is an excellent choice for client projects.
- **Versatile & Professional** – Designed specifically for business websites, making it ideal for client work.
- **Time-Saving Features** – Pre-designed website kits and Hello+ Widgets help you build sites faster without compromising quality.

= Do I need to know how to design? =
Not necessarily, Hello Commerce is designed to make website creation simple and accessible—no design skills required.
- **Intuitive Onboarding Wizard** – Follow the step by step guide to setup your website in just a few clicks.
- **Professionally Designed Templates** – Choose from ready-made layouts tailored to various industries.
- **Easy Customization** – Simply add your content, adjust colors and fonts, and your website is ready to go.

= Can I customize Hello Commerce to fit my brand? =
Absolutely! Hello Commerce offers extensive customization options to ensure your website aligns perfectly with your brand.
- **Intuitive Header & Footer Customization** – Easily adjust layouts, menus, and branding elements.
- **Color Palettes & Typography** – Choose and apply your brand's signature colors and fonts effortlessly.
- **Simplified Styling Options** – Customize your site with user-friendly controls while maintaining a professional look.

= Are there any additional costs to using Hello Commerce? =
Hello Commerce is a free theme available in the WordPress repository. You can enhance your website further by purchasing Elementor Pro, but it is not required for using Hello Commerce.

= Can I upgrade from the Hello Theme to Hello Commerce? =
Yes, you can switch to Hello Commerce without losing any of your work. It's designed to integrate seamlessly with Elementor, so you can continue building and enhancing your site, while benefiting from the new powerful features and tools for a faster workflow.

= If I'm Already Using the Hello Theme, Do I Have to Switch to Hello Commerce? =
No, switching to Hello Commerce is completely optional.
- You can continue using the Hello Theme if it fits your workflow and project needs.
- Switching to Hello Commerce unlocks new features and Hello+ Widgets, making website creation faster and more efficient.

= How can I report security bugs? =
Report security bugs through the Patchstack Vulnerability Disclosure Program. The Patchstack team validates, triages, and handles vulnerabilities. Report a security vulnerability: https://patchstack.com/database/wordpress/theme/kintaelectric/vdp
